WHO ARE WE?

ABOUT US

Ilearnar is a leading adult learning center that offers adults with little or no formal education the opportunity to improve their English communication skills. From a beginner to an advanced learner, we are thrilled to help you read, write, and speak proficiently using standard English.

We also provide advanced communication skills' classes for adults who wish to polish up their English communication skills and speak with confidence.

Our experienced tutors provide engaging and highly personalized instructions to help learners meet their learning needs within the shortest possible time. We offer flexible class schedules, so you can learn at your own pace. Join our community of learners today and experience the power of transformative learning!

VALUES

Excellence: Strive for excellence in teaching and learner support.

Empathy: Show genuine empathy and tailor our approach to learners' unique challenges.

Lifelong Learning: Foster continuous learning and personal growth for learners and our team.

MISSION

To empower adults struggling with English language communication to become proficient, build confidence, and nurture children's public speaking skills through personalized and innovative learning experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you charge?
A: We charge per hour and based on your preferred class type ( physical, online, group or private).
Q: What is the duration of your program?
A: The duration of our program with a learner is absolutely the decision of the learner. However, we may recommend a specific duration for the learner based on the results of their learning needs assessment.
Q: How do you conduct your classes?
A: A learner may choose more than one class type. However, we recommend that those signing up for advanced communication class and public speaking for kids online should schedule time to meet in group to engage the new skills learned and get feedback from each other under their tutorsâ€™ supervision.
Q: What are the minimum requirements for people who wish to join your class?
A: This depends on the class the person wants to join. For the English literacy class, the minimum requirement is the ability to understand common English words (come, go, sit, stand, walk etc). For advanced communication class, the person is assumed to have learned the basic English literacy skills ( reading, writing, speaking and spelling).
Q: Where can I find Ilearnar center?
A: When you you contact us and share your location, we'll direct you to a centre closer to you.
